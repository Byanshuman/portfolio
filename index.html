Professional Mission

I'm Anshuman Yadav — a postgraduate in Psychology. My academic journey has been driven by a deep curiosity about how people think, feel, work, and grow — both individually and within systems. From understanding the human mind to shaping organizational culture, I thrive at the intersection of psychology, people management, and performance development.
