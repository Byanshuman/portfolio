Portfolio & Projects

HR Command Center

About this Project:

The HR Command Center is a centralized suite of interactive tools designed to quantify human capital data. Recognizing that modern HR demands financial literacy, this dashboard enables organizations to translate "soft" HR concepts into "hard" business metrics. It serves as a proof-of-concept for digitizing HR operations without relying on heavy enterprise software.

Key Modules Included:

  • Turnover Costing: Calculating financial risk of attrition.
  • Training ROI: Projecting returns on L&D spend.
  • Bradford Factor: Algorithmic absenteeism tracking.
  • Compa-Ratio: Salary benchmarking & fairness.
  • Recruitment Funnel: Data-driven hiring planning.
  • Interview Generator: Behavioral (STAR) scripts.
